About the Course

This course introduces you to the fascinating field of Corpus Linguistics, where large collections of texts are analyzed to uncover patterns of language use. From building concordances to exploring collocations and discourse structures, you will gain hands-on experience with essential tools like AntConc and Sketch Engine.
