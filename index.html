About the Instructor

Muhammad Shoaib Tahir is a Computational Linguist and Corpus & Computational Linguistics Trainer. He currently serves as the Assistant Manager HR at the Linguistic Association of Pakistan and as a National Resource Person at the Pakistan Corpus Studio.

He has served as a Visiting Lecturer at:

  • Government College University, Faisalabad
  • University of Education, Lahore – Faisalabad Campus
  • University of Agriculture, Faisalabad

He has also been a Resource Person at numerous conferences, seminars, and workshops on Corpus and Computational Linguistics at:

  • UMT Lahore
  • UMT – Sialkot Campus
  • The University of Faisalabad
  • University of Agriculture Faisalabad
  • NUML Faisalabad Campus
  • Air University Islamabad

Through his work, he bridges academic linguistics and computational tools, inspiring learners to explore language data and digital applications in real-world contexts.
