Sample 8-week syllabus (overview)

  1. Week 1: Corpus design, sampling & metadata
  2. Week 2: Text preprocessing & tokenization
  3. Week 3: POS tagging, parsing, and annotation schemas
  4. Week 4: Corpus query, concordances, and frequency analysis
  5. Week 5: NLP pipelines — NER, lemmatization, and embeddings
  6. Week 6: Project planning — research question & dataset
  7. Week 7: Implementation — coding & experiments
  8. Week 8: Portfolio polish & demo presentation
